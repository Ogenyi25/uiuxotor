project overview
project overview
SnackHaven is an e-commerce snack store for snack enthusiasts. It helps users view/search for their favorite snacks, place order and have their mouth watery snack delivered to them within the shortest period of time.
duration: 6 weeks
my role: uiux designer
project type: E-Commerce website
tools used: figma, google forms
Problem Statement
The SnackHaven Factory was stuck in a stale state. While their pastries were undeniably delicious, their online presence was lackluster, failing to capture the imagination and appetite of potential customers.
Their Website
  • Outdated design: Uninspiring visuals and clunky navigation pushed visitors away.
  • Bland copy: Generic descriptions failed to evoke the passion and craftsmanship behind their creations.
  • Limited functionality: Difficulty in ordering and browsing hindered customer engagement.
This resulted in:
  • Declining online sales: The website wasn't attracting new customers or encouraging existing ones to order online.
  • Unmatched reputation: SnackHaven's physical store thrived, but their online presence didn't reflect their true quality and popularity..
  • Missed opportunities: Competitors with stronger online presences were capturing market share.
The Solution
SnackHaven needed a digital makeover to bring their online experience to life, elevate their brand, and finally tap into the vast potential of online pastry orders. To achieve this, I crafted a delectable digital strategy by using Captivating copy, Including a seamless ordering process, and Strategic storytelling.
The result? A website that's as delicious as their pastries:
  • Increased online sales: SnackHaven saw a significant boost in online orders, capturing new customers and boosting convenience for existing ones.
  • Enhanced brand image: The website became a reflection of their true essence, attracting customers who shared their passion for quality and craftsmanship.
  • Competitive edge: SnackHaven established a dominant online presence, solidifying their position in the local market and setting themselves apart from competitors.

Empathize
In the quest to craft the perfect digital experience for SnackHaven, we first embarked on a delicious journey of discovery. We dived headfirst into the world of pastry preferences, eager to understand the desires and pain points of our sweet-toothed target audience. Our mission? To knead together insights that would shape a website as irresistible as their pastries. To get this done, I had to carry out a product research and a competitors analysis.
1. product research
research method
  • Surveys: We surveyed a cross-section of pastry enthusiasts, asking about their buying habits, online ordering preferences, and favorite flavor profiles.
  • In-depth Interview: We delved deeper into the minds of loyal SnackHaven customers, exploring their emotional connection to the brand and their hopes for the online experience.
  • Website Audit: We analyzed the online landscape of Flourish's competitors, evaluating their strengths and weaknesses to identify opportunities for SnackHaven to shine.
  • Market Trends: We researched the latest trends in online pastry ordering, from subscription boxes to personalized recommendations, to stay ahead of the curve.
key findings
  • Convinience Cravings: Customers yearned for a seamless online ordering process, with options for delivery, pickup, and subscription services.
  • Visual Feast: High-quality product photography and enticing visuals were crucial in driving online orders and triggering those mouthwatering cravings.
  • Story Telling Matters: Customers connected with brands that told a compelling story, highlighting the passion and craftsmanship behind their creations.
  • Beyond Bakery Basics: While classic flavors remained popular, customers were excited by unique offerings and seasonal specials that added an element of surprise and delight.
outcome
This in-depth exploration into pastry preferences yielded a treasure trove of insights. We identified the needs and desires of SnackHaven's target audience, laying the foundation for a website that would not only meet their expectations but exceed their wildest pastry dreams.
2. competitors analysis
Analysis Framework
  • Website Design and Functionality: We evaluated the user experience, navigation, and ordering process of each competitor's website.
  • Product Offerings: We compared the variety, quality, and uniqueness of their pastry selections.
  • Pricing and Promotions: We analyzed their pricing strategies, discounts, and special offers.
  • Branding and Storytelling: We assessed how effectively each competitor communicated their brand story and connected with their audience.
key findings
  • Generic Glut: Many competitors' websites lacked personality and struggled to differentiate themselves.
  • Ordering Obstacles: Confusing navigation and cumbersome checkout processes hindered purchase completion.
  • Uniqueness Gap: Limited variety and uninspired flavor profiles failed to excite customers.
  • Storytelling Shortcomings: Generic descriptions and weak brand narratives failed to create an emotional connection.
outcome
This competitive analysis armed us with invaluable insights. We identified the vulnerabilities of SnackHaven's competitors and discovered clear opportunities to carve out a unique and irresistible online space for their brand. For a website that would not only meet their expectations but exceed their wildest pastry dreams.
Define
In the define phase of our SnackHaven journey, we weren't just designing a website; we were crafting an experience. And who better to guide us than Reggie, a user persona embodying the heart and soul of SnackHaven's ideal customer?
User Story:
As a busy professional who loves quality pastries and supporting local businesses, I want to be able to easily order my favorite treats from Flourish online with convenient delivery, so I can indulge in delicious desserts without sacrificing my precious time. Bonus: I'd love it if the website surprised me with unique flavor combinations and seasonal specials, and made me feel like part of a passionate community that appreciates good food.
Reggie's Demographic:
  • Age: 32, young professional with a busy schedule
  • Personality: Warm, adventurous, and passionate about quality food
  • Pastry Preferences: Craves classic comfort with a twist, loves supporting local businesses
  • Online Habits: Tech-savvy but time-pressed, values convenience and visual inspiration
Reggie's Pain Point
  • Clunky Ordering: Struggles with long wait times and confusing checkout processes on other bakery websites.
  • Bland Browsing: Uninspired visuals and generic descriptions fail to ignite her pastry passion.
  • Limited Option: Yearns for unique flavor combinations and seasonal specials that go beyond the basic croissant.
  • Disjointed Experience: Feels disconnected from the brand's story and the passion behind their creations.
Reggie's Goals
  • Seamless Ordering: Ordering her favorite treats should be as effortless as taking a bite of a perfectly flaky pastry.
  • Sensory Enticement: High-quality photography and captivating stories should transport her into a world of Flourish's culinary delights.
  • Curated Discoveries: Personalized recommendations and seasonal specials should introduce her to unexpected flavor treasures.
  • Emotional Connection: Feeling a sense of community and shared passion with the Flourish team.
Ideate
Understanding Reggie's desires and frustrations paved the way for designing a website that catered to his every pastry whim. To achieved this, I focused on:
  • Intuitive navigation and streamlined ordering: Making SnackHaven's website as welcoming and easy to use as their physical store.
  • Mouthwatering visuals and evocative storytelling: Painting a picture of SnackHaven's passion and artistry, making every product page a feast for the eyes.
  • Personalized recommendations and exclusive offers: Keeping Reggie excited and engaged with tailored suggestions and seasonal surprises.
  • Building a community: Weaving SnackHaven's story into the website, showcasing their team and fostering a sense of belonging for customers like Reggie.
By understanding Reggie, we were able to design a website that wasn't just a digital storefront, but a portal to pure pastry paradise.
Design
With a treasure trove of insights and Reggie's sweet guidance, I entered the design phase ready to knead, roll, and shape SnackHaven's digital experience into something truly delectable. This wasn't just about pixels or shapes and images; it was about crafting a website that felt as warm, inviting, and irresistible as a freshly baked pastry.
key design step
  • Information Architecture: I carefully mapped out the website's structure and navigation, ensuring every page was accessible and intuitive, guiding customers smoothly to their desired treats.
  • Wireframing: I sketched out the initial blueprints for each page, focusing on content hierarchy, user flows, and essential functionalities.
  • User Testing: I invited fellow pastry enthusiasts (and Reggie, of course) to test our wireframes and provide feedback. Their insights helped me refined the design and guarantee a seamless user experience.
  • Visual Design: I infused the website with SnackHaven's unique brand personality, incorporating mouthwatering photography, warm color palettes, and playful typography that conveyed their passion and craftsmanship.
  • Responsive Design: I ensured a seamless experience across all devices, from desktop to mobile, so customers could indulge in pastries on the go.
design decisions
  • Hero Banner with Rotating Seasonal Delights: A captivating hero banner showcasing SnackHaven's signature pastries, rotating with seasonal specials to create a sense of anticipation and excitement.
  • Clear Navigation with Mouthwatering Categories: Intuitive navigation with mouthwatering category names to guide pastry explorations effortlessly.
  • Product Page Perfection: Each product page features high-quality imagery, evocative descriptions, and clear calls to action, making ordering as tempting as a freshly frosted cupcake.
  • Seamless Ordering Process: A buttery-smooth ordering experience with secure payment options, convenient delivery/pickup choices, and clear order tracking.
